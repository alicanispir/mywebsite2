July 2024

Digital Twin Development of a R134a Plate-Tube Evaporator

Presented at ASME Heat Transfer Conference 2024. This paper introduces a dynamic digital twin model for a plate-tube evaporator operating with R134a, validated against experimental results and developed using MATLAB Simscape.

August 2024

Digital Twin of a Full-Scale Industrial Heat Pump Producing Steam above 140°C

Published in the 16th IIR-Gustav Lorentzen Conference on Natural Refrigerants. This work explores AI-enhanced modeling of an industrial-scale steam heat pump for high-temperature applications in decarbonized industrial processes.
